Case

vet app for rapid test

this case study focused on how vets diagnose cancer in dogs. The goal was to design an application which could visualize result from a rapid test. We worked with a company which had a new technique of measuring blood, the new technique makes it possible to catch some types of cancer in an earlier stage.

Solution

The vet takes a blood sample and drop blood on the rapid test. Then he/she wait 10 minutes and then they point the mobile camera towards the rapid test. Finally, the screen will show result that can be transferred to the journal.

Today vets take blood sample and send them to a lab and haft to wait for result. With a rapid test and an app for viewing result the procedure would be faster and more efficient.

Treasure

Acceptance is built on user background, experience and the perceived usefulness and usability of the product. The interface needs to be simple and only have the function needed for the vets to reach their goal. It is important with specificity in the test, we also found that the image of the company behind the product is important.

Case

my forest drone application

The project was given by The Swedish forest agency. We developed a design of an interface for a mobile application that helps forest workers to plan and overview the forest. One of our main goals was to motivate workers to use drones in their workday. It was important to develop an interface that forest workers could understand.

Solution

With the application the user can overview the forest with the drone, and tagg objects that he/she are interested in marking. The app has only the most critical information needed for flying, such as battery strength, height of drone and direction of the drone. The application has different map layers because the map layers view different information.

Treasure

it is important to feel in control. Workers in the forest are not use to new technology and needs an application that is easy to use and understand. The project owner had a plan of gathering image material to train a AI of identifying objects when flying. However there is a fear of feeling watched from users. Users could feel less afraid if they had more control over what is happening. Use of transparency is one way of giving more control to users.
